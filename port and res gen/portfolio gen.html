About Me

With a Master of Architecture (M.Arch) and a background in Environmental Design (B.EnvD), my work is rooted in a deep understanding of human-centered and conscious design principles. My academic journey and professional experience have been driven by a fascination with futurism, psychology, and philosophy.

As the VP - Design at Adaptability Canada, I lead initiatives that champion inclusive and accessible environments. I believe in designing not just for the present, but for a future where technology and humanity coexist harmoniously.

Outside of my professional life, I'm a multi-instrumentalist, a passionate woodworker, and an avid gamer. These pursuits fuel my creativity and provide new perspectives that I bring back into my design work.
