Branding & Visual Identity

Moving beyond generic nautical tropes into a curated, "vintage-modern" aesthetic that celebrates the nostalgia of the first summer job.

Brand Essence

The Vibe

High-energy, curated fun, and intentionally nostalgic. We want Sam's to feel like the best part of a 1980s summer camp, updated for 2026.

Visual Palette

A vibrant, multi-color system that allows for dynamic application across digital and physical touchpoints.

Physical Touchpoints

The Space

  • Vintage Signage: Hand-painted style with modern neon accents.
  • Staff Uniforms: Classic ringer tees and high-quality aprons.
  • Packaging: Custom-stamped compostable containers.

The Experience

  • Activity Hub: Clear, vibrant booking station for SUPs/Kayaks.
  • Social Zones: Ambient lighting and curated playlists for evening bistro vibes.
  • Retail Corner: "Sam's Originals" merch and local curiosities.
